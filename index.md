I am a **Senior Adviser** at the **Bank of England**, with a PhD in Economics and Finance from Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona. In my research, I use structural models and causal inference to study financial markets, household finance, and the economic impact of AI. I also use these methods to evaluate how AI systems behave in high-stakes domains.

I am co-organising a conference on Transformative Artificial Intelligence (AI): Implications for Structural Change and Monetary Policy to be held in person in London on 23-24 June, 2026. Please register [here](https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/events/2026/june/transformative-artificial-intelligence) to attend.
